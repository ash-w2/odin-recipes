Thai Green Curry

Description

Don't call the local takeaway â€“ impress friends and family by cooking this fragrant and creamy Thai green chicken curry. It's easy and quick to make

Ingredients

  1. 225g new potatoes, cut into chunks
  2. 100g green beans, trimmed and halved
  3. 1 tbsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  4. 1 garlic clove, chopped
  5. 1 rounded tbsp or 4 tsp Thai green curry paste (you can't fit the tablespoon into some of the jars)
  6. 400ml can coconut milk
  7. 2 tsp Thai fish sauce
  8. 1 tsp caster sugar
  9. 450g boneless skinless chicken (breasts or thighs), cut into bite-size pieces
  10. 2 lime leaves finely shredded, or 3 wide strips lime zest, plus extra to garnish
  11. good handful of basil leaves
  12. boiled rice, to serve
